# Business Requirements / KPI Definitions

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze pizza sales data and extract meaningful business insights related to revenue, customer ordering behavior, and product performance using SQL, Python, and Power BI.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

1. **Total Revenue**
   Calculate the total revenue generated from all pizza orders.

2. **Average Order Value**
   Determine the average amount spent per order by dividing total revenue by the number of orders.

3. **Total Pizzas Sold**
   Calculate the total quantity of pizzas sold.

4. **Total Orders**
   Determine the total number of customer orders placed.

5. **Average Pizzas per Order**
   Calculate the average number of pizzas sold in each order.

## Dashboard Requirements

To better understand sales trends and customer preferences, the following visualizations are included:

1. **Daily Trend of Total Orders**
   A bar chart showing how order volume changes across different days of the week.

2. **Monthly Trend of Total Orders**
   A line chart representing order trends across months to identify seasonal patterns.

3. **Sales Distribution by Pizza Category**
   A pie/donut chart displaying the percentage contribution of each pizza category to total sales.

4. **Sales Distribution by Pizza Size**
   A pie/donut chart showing customer preference for different pizza sizes.

5. **Total Pizzas Sold by Category**
   A funnel or bar chart comparing sales volume across pizza categories.

6. **Top 5 Best Sellers**
   Identify the top 5 pizzas based on:

   * Revenue
   * Quantity Sold
   * Number of Orders

7. **Bottom 5 Sellers**
   Identify the 5 lowest-performing pizzas based on:

   * Revenue
   * Quantity Sold
   * Number of Orders
